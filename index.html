INSTRUCTIONS TO FILL THE ONLINE FORM FOR END SEMESTER (REGULAR /RETEST) EXAMINATION 2020


  1. PORTAL is  www.scteassam.in
  2. Click on Registration.
  3. Fill up the registration form
  4. After completion of form fill up click on Confirm and register button
  5. After successful registration an Application reference number (ARN) will be generated. Please Note the ARN Number
  6. Click on log in button.
  7. User name will be your ARN no. After entering password log in
  8. Click on examination Registration and then submit button.
  9. Scan copy of signature and photo must be uploaded by student
  10. Click on grade report to view your grade report
